My Journey to SSN


I started my education in an elementary school near my village. I studied there till grade 5th maintaining top grades. Due to good grades I was selected from my school to give entrance exam to VidyaGyan School (Sitapur,UP), a charitable school for meritorious student with poor economic background. I got 100% scholarship to study in VG. I passed Higher secondary(10th) with 10 CGPA. I chose Science, Maths and IP in 11th due to my interest in Mathematics and Coding. I cleared Senior Secondary(12th) with 92.2% marks. After clearing Aptitude and Interview round, I was admitted to SSN College of Engineering with 100% scholarship. Currently i am pursuing my undergraduation in BE Computer Science and Engineering with current CGPA of 8.8.
